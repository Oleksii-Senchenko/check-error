Aviation Data Solutions are pleased to announce the upcoming launch of AvOS (Aviation Operations Software); a new low-cost, lightweight yet highly-capable ground operations software platform for ground handlers, into-plane agents and airports

What is AvOS?

AvOS is a lightweight, low-cost modular ground operations software with a strong emphasis on environmental impact awareness. Its USPs are:

  • A unique solution that spans the three main ground activities at an airport: ground handling, airport operations and refuelling. Organisations can take a “pick and mix” approach to choose the module(s) that are relevant for their operational needs.

  • An easy to use software solution with an intuitive user interface, with minimal training needed to gain maximum benefit from all of the features.

  • A cost-effective solution for organisations currently using manual processes to realise the full benefits and efficiencies of well-designed and connected operations software.

  • The first operations software solution to place an emphasis on quantifying the environmental impact of flights supported, which is enabled by ADS’s proprietary aircraft and engine database.

The initial release version of AvOS will be the ground handling module only, with the planned airport operations and into-plane modules to follow later
